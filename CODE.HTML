Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NFTverse

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. It's a unique digital asset that represents ownership of a specific item or piece of content, stored on a blockchain. Unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin which are identical and interchangeable, each NFT is unique and can't be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.

NFTverse NFTs are stored on the Ethereum blockchain, which provides a secure and transparent record of ownership. When you purchase an NFT from our collection, you're buying a unique token that proves your ownership of that specific digital artwork.

To mint an NFT from our collection:

  1. Connect your Ethereum-compatible wallet (like MetaMask) to our website
  2. Ensure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the mint price and gas fees
  3. Click the "Mint Now" button during the minting period
  4. Select the number of NFTs you wish to mint (maximum 10 per transaction)
  5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  6. After the transaction is confirmed, your NFTs will appear in your wallet

You can then view your NFTs on OpenSea or other compatible marketplaces.

We support all Ethereum-compatible wallets including:

  • MetaMask (Browser extension and mobile app)
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • Trust Wallet
  • WalletConnect compatible wallets
  • Ledger (via MetaMask)
  • Trezor (via MetaMask)

For the best experience, we recommend using MetaMask. Make sure your wallet is connected to the Ethereum Mainnet before minting.

NFTverse consists of 10,000 unique NFTs with the following pricing structure:

  • Presale (Whitelist only): 0.05 ETH per NFT (limit 5 per wallet)
  • Public Sale: 0.08 ETH per NFT (limit 10 per transaction)

There will be a total of 2,000 NFTs available during the presale and 8,000 during the public sale. All NFTs are randomly generated at the time of minting.

NFTverse holders enjoy several benefits:

  • Access to exclusive holder-only Discord channels
  • Participation in community governance votes
  • Eligibility for periodic airdrops of new NFTs
  • Early access to future NFTverse collections
  • Commercial usage rights for your NFTs
  • Invitations to real-world and virtual events
  • Staking rewards (coming Q3 2023)

Additional benefits will be announced as we progress through our roadmap.
